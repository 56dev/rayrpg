Functions and structs supplied by the library are prefixed with RRPG_. Additional prefixes may further indicate the "namespaces" the function or struct is in.
Functions are in snake_case, while structs are in PascalCase. 
Global variables that are constant or only intended to be changed once are SCREAMING_SNAKE_CASE.
Fields and functions intended to be accessed only by the library are not prefixed with RRPG, but rather with xxprv.
